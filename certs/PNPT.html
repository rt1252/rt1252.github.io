Quick Info

  • Official link: https://certifications.tcm-sec.com/pnpt/
  • Study time for me ~2 months
  • Exam time 5 days
  • Exam price $499 (no other materials needed)
  • Difficulty: 6/10

Background Info

This is the second TCM Security exam that I have taken, I passed PJPT a few months before taking this test.

This exam is a five day practical exam with two more days for reporting.

Study Materials

Do not bother studying anything on this exam other than the PEH course, absolutely nothing else is required.

You need to FULLY understand important topics from the course including finding external attack vectors, pivoting, and AD attacks.

Exam Experience

This is pretty embarrassing, failed twice at the same point because of my inability to pay attention to detail.

Exam experience was very smooth no VPN or connection issues, they have support but don't expect to get anything from them.

If you do a full reset on the exam expect to wait 10-20 minutes before everything is back online.

Exam Review

Very good foundational content for learning AD attacks, I would highly recommend the course and exam especially at the very cheap price point.

The exam report portion was annoying to format in Word, but making a report with sufficient detail is another realistic portion of their exam.

Luckily the exam is gaining some traction on Indeed, normally about 10 listings including it on indeed search for remote positions, it has appeared as a preferred qualification for companies like Microsoft & McDonalds.
