TCM Security's PNPT

TCM Security’s PNPT

Jump to sections: Quick Info · Background Info · Study Materials · Exam Experience · Exam Review

Quick Info

  • Official link: https://certifications.tcm-sec.com/pnpt/
  • Study time for me ~2 months
  • Exam time: 5 days
  • Exam price: $499 (no other materials needed)
  • Difficulty: 6/10
  • Date Earned: 2024-09-16
  • Credential Link: credential

Background Info

This is the second TCM Security exam that I have taken; I passed PJPT a few months before taking this test.

This exam is a five-day practical exam with two more days for reporting.

Study Materials

Do not bother studying anything on this exam other than the PEH course — absolutely nothing else is required.

You need to fully understand topics from the course including finding external attack vectors, pivoting, and AD attacks.

Exam Experience

This is pretty embarrassing — I failed twice at the same point because of lack of attention to detail.

Exam experience was smooth with no VPN or connection issues; support exists but may not be helpful. If you do a full reset expect to wait 10–20 minutes for services to come back online.

Exam Review

Very good foundational content for learning AD attacks; I would highly recommend the course and exam at this price point.

The exam report portion can be annoying to format in Word, but producing a sufficiently detailed report is an important skill.
