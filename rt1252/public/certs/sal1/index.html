TryHackMe's SAL1

TryHackMe’s SAL1

Jump to sections: Quick Info · Background Info · Study Materials · Exam Experience · Exam Review

Quick Info

  • Official link: https://tryhackme.com/certification/security-analyst-level-1
  • Study time for me: < 3 hours
  • Exam time: ~5 hours
  • Exam cost for me: $0 (free upon release for CySA+ & BTL1 holders)
  • Difficulty: 4/10
  • Date Earned: 2025-03-09
  • Credential Link: credential

Background Info

This is THM’s first exam which simulates a SOC analyst’s responsibilities.

The exam consists of 80 multiple choice, then two SOC simulations each two hours long.

Study Materials

Already holding CompTIA certifications similar to this such as CySA+, I put only around 3 hours of studying before taking the test.

I would recommend doing the Splunk modules from the recommended learning path if you have never used Splunk or need a quick refresher.

Additionally, use the SOC simulator and do the Phishing Unfolded challenge to get experience using the simulator and see how the AI wants your case reports to look.

Exam Experience

The multiple choice section can be completed quickly; the SOC simulator allows opening multiple tabs which is helpful.

If you have SOC experience you’ll find the alerts easier, but be thorough in your reporting — the AI grader looks for detail (5Ws, evidence from Splunk, and overall context).

Only TP case reports are graded; mark FPs as FP so you don’t waste time writing reports for false positives.

Exam Review

Overall the exam was smooth and I enjoyed investigating alerts in Splunk. This was a good SOC exam that actual tested your ability to investigate alerts unlike CySA+.

My main critique is uncertainty around how the AI grader works; sometimes I copied parts of previous reports because I wasn’t sure if the grader would correlate them.
