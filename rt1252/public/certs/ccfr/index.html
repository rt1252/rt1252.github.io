Quick Info

  • Official link: https://www.crowdstrike.com/learn/training/certifications/
  • Study time for me: ~ 3-4 weeks
  • Exam time: 30 minutes
  • Exam price: $0 (CS alliance through employment)
  • Difficulty: [4/10]
  • Date Earned: 2025-07-11
  • Credential Link: credential

Background Info

During my time working on a SOC team my first 6 months of full-time employment, I decided to get another credential to supplement my feedback cycle. My SOC team used CrowdStrike for investigating alerts and I had become familiar investigating alerts on the platform.

The training and certification were covered due to my employer having an aliance with CrowdStrike. I do not think I would have paid for this certificate out of pocket, it does not hold weight in industry.

Study Materials

The materials from the CCFR path in CrowdStrike University were enough to pass the exam.

Exam Experience

The exam was pretty easy and the training material covered a lot of the exam material. The stuff that was not covered was common knowledge for those who use CS.
