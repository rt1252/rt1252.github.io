CompTIA Security+

Jump to sections: Quick Info 路 Background Info 路 Study Materials 路 Exam Experience 路 Exam Review

Quick Info

  • Official link: https://www.comptia.org/certifications/security
  • Study time for me: 7 weeks
  • Exam time: 1 hour
  • Exam price: ~ $400
  • Difficulty: [6/10]
  • Date Earned: 2023-07-29
  • Credential Link: credential

Background Info

At the time of taking this exam this was my first real cybersecurity certification that holds some weight in industry.

I took this certification at the end of my junior year summer internship, using free time at work to study for this exam.

Study Materials

  • My study schedule, make a copy of the notion page!
  • Sybex Security+ Study Guide, the study guide includes access to the Wiley testbank which was super valuable.
  • Dion Training Practice Exam
  • Professor Messer Practice Exam

Exam Experience

The exam was pretty straightforward, I went in person to the testing center. I would always recommend doing this as the online proctors can be annoying to deal with. I was well prepared following the study schedule and was able to pass first attempt.

Exam Review

No complaints with this exam as it does open doors up for internships and entry level positions. The price is a bit steep for being a multiple choice exam, but it is what it is.
